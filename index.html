Toronto, QC | May 3-4

Learn More Register Now

Why Brainhack ?

Brainhack is a collaborative event that focuses on the open sharing of ideas, code, and data. During Brainhack Global, we'll meet in Toronto to work on projects in coordination with other Brainhack sites around the world. Join us on Slack to connect with the community and start planning your project ! Our channel name is #brainhack-toronto


Dates: May 3-4, 2018
Cost: C$30 for registration with onsite breakfast & lunch
Location: SciNet Classrooms, 11th floor, MaRS

Learn

Learn about tools, techniques and datasets in neuroimaging research.

Be Social

Meet researchers from a variety of backgrounds. Share your knowledge.

Get Involved

Find exciting projects that you would like to contribute to.

Schedule:

The event will start Thursday morning at 9a with breakfast, registration, and introductions. The rest of Thursday and all day Friday will be dedicated to hacking on projects, with tutorials and break-out/unconference sessions happening as needed. Early-evening on Friday, there will be a wrap-up session with a short presentation from each project on their achievements.

Due to the nature of the event, the schedule is flexible and can change frequently. The only confirmed events will be breakfast at 9a and lunch at noon both days. We are also planning an optional pub-outing Thursday evening around 5p.

To stay up-to-date, you can subscribe to our Google calendar.
